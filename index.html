The Bar

The cocktail bar plays an essential role in the sustainable philosophy and zero waste approach. Unused herbs and vegetables survive in infusions and fermentations. The farmer-botanist, the mixologist and the chef work closely together to link the cocktails on the tasting menu with seasonal ingredients.

The cocktail bar offers seats for cocktails before and after dinner (6pm-7.30pm and after 10.30pm).

European biodynamic wines and artisanal beers from Belgian microbreweries reflect the same love of terroir and ethical agriculture.

Creativity
